 # Superstore Sales Dashboard & Forecast (Power BI)
# Project Summary

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends for a retail superstore. It also includes a 15-day sales forecast to support short-term business planning and decision-making.

# Key Highlights

Analyzed $1.57M in total sales, 22K orders, and $175K profit

Identified top-performing categories and sub-categories

Visualized regional and state-wise sales distribution

Compared monthly sales and profit year-over-year (2019 vs 2020)

Built a 15-day sales forecast to predict near-term demand

# Business Insights

Office Supplies generated the highest revenue, followed by Technology and Furniture

Phones, Chairs, and Binders were the top-selling sub-categories

The West region contributed the highest share of sales

California, New York, and Texas were the top-performing states

Consumer segment accounted for nearly half of total sales

Standard Class was the most-used shipping mode

Online payments were the most preferred payment method
